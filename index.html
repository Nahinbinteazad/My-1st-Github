Dr. Muhammad Yunus, a Nobel Peace Prize laureate, envisions a future where social businesses replace charity, creating a world where profit is used to solve societal problems. He imagines a society where poverty and unemployment are eradicated through innovative and sustainable solutions. Yunus advocates for a shift in economic focus, emphasizing people over profit, and believes in empowering individuals to take charge of their destinies. His vision is one where global challenges are addressed through collaboration, innovation, and collective responsibility.
